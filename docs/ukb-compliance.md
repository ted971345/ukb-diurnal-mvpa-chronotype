# UK Biobank Compliance Notes

This repository is intended to share **analysis code only**.

## Do not share
- Any UK Biobank participant-level data (raw or derived).
- Any datasets containing participant identifiers (e.g., `eid`) or linkable quasi-identifiers.
- Any participant-level model diagnostics or exports (e.g., row-level tables, individual predictions).

## Allowed to share (generally)
- Analysis scripts and documentation.
- Aggregated summary tables that do not enable re-identification.
- Figures based on aggregate statistics.

Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with their UK Biobank Material Transfer Agreement and data access conditions.
